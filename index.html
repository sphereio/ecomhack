About

This weekend in Berlin is all about projects with an interesting approach to personalization and fresh ideas that make buying things more individual, more effective - and more fun.

  • - Get together in teams of up to 5 people and create amazing commerce hacks
  • - Forget manuals and documentation - code in lightspeed with API experts from Google, PayPal and others by your side
  • - Present your project in front of a jury of internationally renowned experts and win prizes worth more than 60.000 €

If you are:

  • An expert e-commerce developer
  • An experienced designer
  • A creative idea generator
  • Or a skilled shopping queen ;-)

Then this event is the perfect opportunity for you to:

  • Mingle and share experience with awesome people
  • Work with the hottest APIs in the industry
  • Show your skills and expand your horizon
  • Enjoy lots of great food & drinks

Rules

  • 1. Start your project from scratch at the first day of the event.
  • 2. Work on an idea related to the topic "Personal Commerce".
  • 3. Include SPHERE.IO and any other of the participating APIs in your concept.
  • 4. Team size 1 - 5 people.
  • 5. Powerpoint-free zone: It’s your demo that’s relevant - leave the flashy slides to the consultants;) If it’s not fully functional (yet), just walk us through the code.
