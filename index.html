Manifesto

  • We believe that new developments in hard- and software cause a major paradigm shift in the industry.
  • We believe that as a developer community we can take an active role in shaping the future of commerce.
  • We believe that true innovation comes from an exchange of ideas, knowledge sharing and collaboration.
  • We believe that an API ecosystem and the free flow of data has the highest potential for a disruption on a global level.
  • We believe that we can improve people’s lives by using smart technologies.
