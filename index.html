Manifesto

  1. 1. We believe that new developments in hard- and software cause a major paradigm shift in the industry.
  2. 2. We believe that as a developer community we can take an active role in shaping the future of commerce.
  3. 3. We believe that true innovation comes from an exchange of ideas, knowledge sharing and collaboration.
  4. 4. We believe that an API ecosystem and the free flow of data has the highest potential for a disruption on a global level.
  5. 5. We believe that we can improve people’s lives by using smart technologies.
