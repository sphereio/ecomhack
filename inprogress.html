Personal Commerce
9-10 May Berlin (Germany)
register

Providing a personal shopping experience has always been one of the most important drivers of commerce. In the pre-webstore days, shop owners knew their customers and could offer a tailor-made selection. These days, online merchants leverage disruptive developments in digital technology to offer a similar quality of personal service and scale it to reach a global audience. Emerging devices enable new forms of personalized interaction and redefine the way in which online retail is done. ecomhack 2015 is all about innovative approaches to individualized e-commerce scenarios and the role of APIs.
Providing individualized shopping experiences is one of the most important drivers of commerce in the future - and has been in the past.
